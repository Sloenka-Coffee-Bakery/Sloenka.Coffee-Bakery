Our Story

Crafted with Care, Baked with Love

At Sloenka, we believe that the best moments happen over a warm cup of coffee and a freshly baked pastry. What started as a small passion project has grown into a beloved community hub.

We source our beans from ethical farms around the world and mill our flour locally. Every loaf of sourdough, every flaky croissant, and every pour-over is made with intention and a whole lot of heart.
